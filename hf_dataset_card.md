> ⚠️ **Synthetic Data Disclaimer**: This dataset contains **synthetically generated data** for demonstration and testing purposes. It does not represent real building measurements or actual climate conditions.

## Overview

Real-time HVAC and climate telemetry data for building energy management systems. This dataset provides indoor/outdoor environmental conditions, HVAC status, and 24-hour trends.
